Any proposal submitted in response to this solicitation should be submitted in accordance with the revised NSF Proposal & Award Policies & Procedures Guide (PAPPG) that is in effect for the relevant funding opportunity.

SYNOPSIS OF PROGRAM

The Growing Research Access for Nationally Transformative Equity and Diversity (GRANTED) program focuses on improving research support and service capacity at emerging research institutions. Proposals are invited that develop, scale, or disseminate research-support services that expand participation in federally funded research. The program welcomes proposals from two- and four-year institutions of higher education, non-profit organizations, and consortia thereof.

ELIGIBILITY

Limited to institutions that meet the definition of an Emerging Research Institution as specified in the CHIPS and Science Act. Individual investigators must hold an appointment at an eligible institution at the time of proposal submission.

PROPOSAL PREPARATION AND SUBMISSION INSTRUCTIONS

A. Proposal Preparation Instructions — Full Proposals submitted via Research.gov or Grants.gov must follow all requirements of the PAPPG Chapter II.
B. Budgetary Information — The maximum total budget including indirect costs is $500,000 over up to three years.

National Science Board approved merit review criteria (Intellectual Merit and Broader Impacts) apply, along with program-specific solicitation review criteria specified below.
