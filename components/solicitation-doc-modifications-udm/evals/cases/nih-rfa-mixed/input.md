## Solicitation

```markdown
# Request for Applications: Exploratory Studies in Rare Disease Mechanisms (Synthetic)

RFA-XX-25-SYN

This Request for Applications (RFA) uses the R01 mechanism with modifications appropriate to its exploratory scope. All instructions in the NIH Application Instructions for Research apply except as modified below.

## Section IV. Application and Submission Information

### Research Strategy page limit

The Research Strategy is limited to 6 pages, reflecting the exploratory scope of this RFA. Applicants should prioritize a well-defined hypothesis and a focused Approach; extensive Preliminary Studies are not expected. All other Research Strategy requirements, including Significance / Innovation / Approach subsections and embedded Preliminary Studies, remain in effect.

### Milestones and Timeline (required supplement)

All applications must include a Milestones and Timeline supplement of no more than 2 pages describing quantitative milestones for each Specific Aim, expected completion dates by quarter, go / no-go decision points, and contingency plans for milestones that are not met. This supplement is uploaded in the "Other Attachments" slot and is separate from the Research Strategy page limit.

No other modifications to the standard NIH R01 application package apply under this RFA.
```
