## Solicitation

```markdown
# Dear Colleague Letter: Focused Investigator Awards in Integrative Ecology

NSF DCL-SYN-01

This Dear Colleague Letter supplements the standard NSF Proposal & Award Policies & Procedures Guide for proposals submitted in response to the Focused Investigator Awards in Integrative Ecology pilot. Except as modified below, all standard PAPPG requirements apply.

## Modifications to standard PAPPG requirements

- **Project Description.** The Project Description must not exceed 10 pages. All other PAPPG requirements for the Project Description, including the treatment of Results from Prior NSF Support within the page limit, remain unchanged.

- **Data Management Plan.** The Data Management Plan must not exceed 3 pages and must include a required subheading titled "Sample and Specimen Sharing" addressing the disposition of physical samples, voucher specimens, and associated metadata. The standard PAPPG Data Management Plan elements remain required.

No other documents required by the PAPPG are modified by this DCL, and this DCL introduces no additional documents.
```
