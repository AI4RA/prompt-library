```markdown
# NSF Engineering Research Center Program Solicitation (Synthetic Excerpt)

NSF 99-SYN-ERC

## Additional required documents

Proposals submitted to this program must include the standard PAPPG document set, with the following program-specific supplementary documents uploaded as separate files in the "Other Supplementary Documents" slot. These requirements are in addition to, and do not modify, the standard PAPPG requirements.

### Strategic Plan

A Strategic Plan of no more than 5 pages describing the proposed Center's vision, strategic goals, performance metrics, leadership structure, and the relationship between the research thrusts and the Center's long-term objectives. The plan must include the following required subheadings: "Vision and Goals", "Performance Metrics", "Leadership and Management Structure", and "Thrust Integration".

### Knowledge Transfer Plan

A Knowledge Transfer Plan of no more than 3 pages describing how research outcomes will be translated into practice, including industrial partnerships, technology transfer mechanisms, workforce development activities, and intellectual property strategy. The plan must name the proposed Industrial Advisory Board chair and describe the Center's process for engaging member companies.
```
